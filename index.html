How does it work?

The Cellfish charger converts kinetic energy to power you can use to charge your smartphone or other devices. It fits in your pocket and doesn't weight much, making it easy to bring with you. You can carry it in your pocket and around your arm, or attach it to your bicycle wheel. It also comes with multiple USB outputs, allowing you to charge multiple devices at the same time.
